About

I am an undergrad student at The University of Colorado Boulder in the College of Engineering and Applied Science, studying Computer Science. I am driven by a love for data and a fascination with the math behind it. I am especially interested in the intersect between data and linear algebra and its applications to modern technology such as AI. Apart from that, I am also heavily interested in problem solving with algorithms and logic, as well as philosophy. Outside of university, I am an avid coffee enjoyer, rock climber, and cyclist!
